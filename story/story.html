My Story

They call me Jeddy...

( J. + Eddy = Jeddy)

I am an Army veteran, caretaker to two of the craziest doggos in existence, and an aspiring web developer.

My 20's can best be described as a rollercoaster that doesn't necessarily meet safety standards. In the past 7 years, I have joined the military, deployed twice, earned a Bachelor's degree in Business Management/Supply Chain from the University of Central Arkansas, and landed a full-time gig at a transportion company.

After all the time, effort, and push-ups dedicated to obtaining my degree and getting a job in the field I studied, I realized that it just isn't for me. I need to create, to build, to leverage my abilities in logic and problem solving in a way that is more rewarding than counting beans.

Enter, web dev...
